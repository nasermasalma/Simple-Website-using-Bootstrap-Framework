What About Our Course Goals

Knowledge and understanding

Understand the peculiarities of the three paradigms of mobile computing: native, m-sites and hybrid.

Cognitive skills

Construct a mobile strategy to meet enterprise needs and Comprehend real challenges of mobile development at industrial contexts.

Communication skills

Comprehend interaction, presentation and behavioral design patterns and Illustrate the basic building blocks of native apps for Android Nougat

Practical skills

Create native mobile apps for the enterprise using Android platform and Understand how to create and consume scalable RESTful Web Services

Android Vs. iOS Operating System: Which One of The Two is Better or Worse

Android Vs. iOS Operating System: Which One of The Two is Better or Worse

The most common problem which the usual portable devices face is the capacity of RAM. In short, RAM defines the working ability of a smart phone. The function of RAM allows the processor to act quickly and to switch from one process to the other without hesitations and interruption. Since, most of the smart phones are of limited size, a very small difference in the RAM size can make a big difference in the ability of work of the device. Also, the compatibility of RAM with other system specs is a factor which effects the load time and alters the system performance.
